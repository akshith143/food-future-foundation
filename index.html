Transforming Food Systems in India

Towards food secure, safe, healthy and sustainable world

Our Food systems must create a world where everyone has access to safe, nutritious and affordable food. We must ensure that we are all free from hunger and all forms of malnutrition. Biodiversity is sustained; environmental degradation is prevented livelihoods safeguarded. We wish to achieve this by 2030 as a part of the UN Sustainable Development Goals 2030.

Our current food systems not geared to fulfil the vision food secure, safe, healthy and sustainable world.
