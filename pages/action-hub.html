Action Hub

Making it Happen

To amplify the scale & scope of current, working solutions, and to create new realities for a better food future by building partnerships, coalitions and seed communities.

Target Groups

Children
  • To develop lifelong healthy eating habits.
  • To sensitize children to the benefits & challenges of sustainable development in an interconnected world.
  • Develop the ‘Healthy People, Healthy Planet’ initiative, and align with school curricula.
  • Develop model school food labs & school gardens.
  • Support the implementation of food regulations in schools.
Youth
  • To foster leadership & the desire for system-level change in food systems in the leaders of tomorrow.
  • Curate the Food Systems Fellowship Program for candidates under the age of 28, as on 1st April, 2021.





General Citizens
  • To nudge behavioral changes in citizens towards food purchase & consumption.
  • Create “Food-Smart” Schools.
  • Encourage Food Wastage Reduction.
  • Provide guidance on nutrition & balanced diets.
  • Encourage consumer-led testing.
  • Promote “Same Taste, Better Health” initiative through nutrient labelling.
Farmers
  • To build food capacity.
  • To share knowledge & best practices.
  • Share Regenerative & sustainable farming practices.
  • Promote Regenerative Farming through the “Su-Upaj” Network.
  • Promote the use of technology to improve traceability, reduce water wastage, reduce chemical fertilizers & pesticides, and promote local & seasonal economically viable crops.
  • Build a “Back to Roots” Community of professionals going back to farming.
Food environments & networks
  • To promote, share & facilitate best practices in food systems.
  • Form “Once Again Me”, a network to provide a safe space for women.
  • Create the Indian Chef Network (TiCNet).
  • Form a Food Startup Network.
  • Develop Eat Right Districts & Urban Food Plans.
  • Form “DAAN”, an initiative to reduce food loss & wastage through data analysis & research.



Markets
  • To ensure that markets provide safe & nutritious food.
  • Address gaps in credibility & reduce harmful components in food through the Positive Nutrition Lab.
  • Address the issues of food safety, adulteration & fraud through product & process certification and traceability through the Food Authenticity Lab.
  • Develop Food Label Solutions to ensure compliance.
  • Curate FLeP – A Food Labelling e-Learning Program.
