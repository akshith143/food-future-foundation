Coalition of Partners for Food System
Transformation India

The Coalition is a multi-stakeholder forum. It would develop shared vision by aligning the interests of various stakeholders and support contextual implementation of ideas and strategies that come out of the UNFSS Processes and build commitment for collective action for a safe, more nutritious, sustainable and equitable food system. While, doing so, the Coalition will maintain necessary global links to accelerate to accelerate the pace of diffusion of know-how. It has been initiated by the Food Future Foundation with the support of CII Food and Agriculture Centre of Excellence (CII-FACE) and the Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition (GAIN). The Food Future Foundation will function as its secretariat.
